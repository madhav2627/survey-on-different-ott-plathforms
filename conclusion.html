CONCLUSION

Based on the survey conducted among 50 residents, it was found that 18 individuals, accounting for 36% of the population, do not use any OTT platform. This means the remaining 32 residents, or 64%, are users of one or more OTT platforms. Among the platforms used, Hotstar emerged as the most popular with 14 users, followed by Amazon Prime with 9 users. Netflix, ETV Win, and other OTT platforms each have 3 users. This indicates that while a majority of residents are engaged with digital streaming services, there is still a notable portion who do not use any. Hotstar and Amazon Prime dominate the OTT usage, whereas platforms like Netflix and ETV Win have relatively low adoption in this community..
